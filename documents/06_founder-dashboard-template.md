# The Solo Founder Weekly Dashboard
## KPI Tracking, Time Audit, and Reflection System

You can't improve what you don't measure. Most solo founders have a vague sense of whether things are going well, based on gut feel and inbox volume. This dashboard gives you a real, weekly snapshot of the metrics that actually matter — revenue, pipeline, content, AI leverage, and strategic momentum.

It takes 15 minutes per week to fill out. The data compounds. After 12 weeks, you'll have a map of your business that no accounting tool, analytics platform, or advisor can replicate.

## How to Use This Dashboard with AI

Each month, paste your completed dashboard into Claude or ChatGPT and run this prompt:

```
Here is my monthly business dashboard data: [paste data]

Act as a business analyst reviewing this for the first time.
1. What are the 3 most important trends in this data?
2. Which metrics show the most variance — and what could explain it?
3. Where is the biggest gap between my targets and actuals?
4. What would you focus on in the next 30 days based on this data alone?
5. Is there anything in this data that should alarm me, that I might be rationalizing away?
```

The combination of your honest weekly tracking and AI analysis creates a feedback loop that most solo founders never have. Use it consistently and watch how much faster you learn from your own business.
