# Solo Founder AI Operating System

**Run Your Week With AI: Roles, Repeatable Workflows, and Less Mental Clutter**

## Overview

The Solo Founder AI Operating System is a comprehensive digital product designed for solo founders who want to leverage AI strategically to run their business more efficiently. This system provides a simple operating framework for planning, delegating, and reviewing work with AI assistance.

## Why This Product Wins

Solo founders are overwhelmed with managing every aspect of their business. This operating system gives them the structure they need without the complexity of traditional project management tools.

## What's Included

### Core Components

1. **AI Role Map** - Define and assign roles to AI assistants for different aspects of your business
2. **Weekly Planning Template** - Structured weekly planning system to stay organized
3. **Task Routing System** - Know exactly which tasks to delegate to AI vs. handle yourself
4. **Daily Review Checklist** - End-of-day review system to maintain momentum
5. **Prompt Library** - Collection of 25+ proven AI prompts for common founder tasks
6. **Founder Dashboard Worksheet** - Track your key metrics and priorities
7. **Decision Log** - Document important decisions and their context
8. **Output Quality Checklist** - Ensure AI outputs meet your standards
