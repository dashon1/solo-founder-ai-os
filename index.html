Frequently Asked Questions

What format is the product delivered in?
The Starter package is delivered as a high-quality PDF designed for printing and filling out by hand. The Professional package also includes Notion templates and Google Sheets that you can copy directly into your accounts.
Do I need prior experience with AI to use this?
No! This system is designed for founders at any AI experience level. If you've never used AI tools, the Role Map and Prompt Library will get you started. If you're already using AI, the Task Routing System and Quality Checklist will help you be more intentional.
How much time do I need to invest to use this system?
The Weekly Planning Template takes about 30 minutes once a week. The Daily Review Checklist is just 5 minutes at the start and end of each day. The prompts can be used as needed for specific tasks.
What's included in the Notion templates?
The Notion system includes a pre-built AI Role Map database, Weekly Planning board with linked properties, a Task Tracker with routing status, and a Dashboard view with auto-updating metrics from your task database.
What does the AI Workflow Automation Guide include?
The guide includes 10 advanced automation workflows using tools like Zapier and Make, with step-by-step instructions for connecting your AI tools to your existing workflows. It also includes 25+ bonus prompts for power users.
How do I access the product after purchase?
After purchase, you'll receive an instant download link for the PDF. Notion templates and Google Sheets come with copyable template links. The Automation Guide is delivered as an additional PDF.
